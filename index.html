Boiler Tube Replacement

Emergency Boiler Repair at Palm Oil Mill

The Challenge

A palm oil mill experienced sudden boiler tube failure causing complete production shutdown. The client needed urgent repair to resume operations within 7 days.

Assessment

Upon inspection, we identified:

  • Multiple tube failures due to corrosion
  • Scale buildup affecting heat transfer
  • Water treatment system malfunction

The Solution

  • Complete tube replacement for affected section
  • Boiler internal cleaning and descaling
  • Water treatment system recalibration
  • Hydrostatic testing and commissioning
Results
6 days Completion Time
100% Production Restored
30% Efficiency Improved
Duration: 6 days
Industry: Palm Oil Mill
